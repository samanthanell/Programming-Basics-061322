Bios of the Best Shelties Ever:

Piper Artemis Nell and Jamie Winky Nell are two Shetland Sheepdogs who grew up in Winnetka, Il. 7 and 4 respectively, they had lived all of their fluffy lives in tha frozen tundra of Chicago before moving with their owners to the tropical paradise that is Sarasota, FL. While giving up snow was not fun as both enjoy it during the winter as something to bark at and eat, Florida has provided new diversions. They enjoy chasing/hunting geckos, while still participating in their favorite pastimes of barking at the tv and pulling on their owners shoelaces

Jamie's favorite foods are macadamia nuts and oranges, while Piper is partial to apples and popcorn. They are really not supposed to be eating any of these but manage to any way. Dumpster diving is frequently involved
